Our Admission procedure...

BSc : MEDICAL LABORATORY TECHNOLOGY
(10+2) passed or equivalent from Science stream with Biology & Chemistry from any recognized Institution/Board
. 2 year vocational course passed in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT) after 10th standard from a recognized University/Institution/Board and selection will be made by Board as per University guide line.
MSc : MEDICAL BIOINFORMATICS BSc(H) or 3 year BSc Degree with at least 55% from any stream of Biological Sciences, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science, Bioinformatics Statistics, and BTech in Biotechnology, Computer Science, Information Technology and MBBS from any recognized University.
MSc : MEDICAL BIOTECHNOLOGY BSc(H) or 3 year BSc Degree with at least 55% from any stream of Biological Sciences, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science, Bioinformatics Statistics, and BTech in Biotechnology, Computer Science, Information Technology and MBBS from any recognized University.
MSc : HUMAN GENETICS BSc(H) or 3 year BSc Degree with at least 55% from any stream of Biological Sciences and MBBS from recognized University.
